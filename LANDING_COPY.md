## FAQ

### Do I need to connect my billing account?

No. You enter your tools, plans, and monthly spend manually. The audit runs instantly — no OAuth, no invoice uploads, no account required.

### Why do you ask for email after the report, not before?

Because the tool should prove value first. You see the full audit and savings breakdown before we ask for anything. Email is only used to send your report and follow up if there's a significant savings opportunity.

### Is my public report private?

Yes. The shareable URL strips your company name, email, and role. It shows tools, plans, spend numbers, and recommendations only — safe to share with your team or post publicly.

### Does AI decide the recommendations?

No. Every recommendation is deterministic — hardcoded rules traced back to official vendor pricing pages, verified by date. AI only writes the short summary paragraph at the top. The math is not AI-generated.

### When does Credex show up?

Only when your estimated savings exceed $500/month. Below that, Credex can't materially help you and we don't pretend otherwise. Above it, discounted AI credits become a real option worth knowing about.
