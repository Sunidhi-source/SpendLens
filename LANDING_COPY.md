## FAQ

### Do I need to connect billing?

No. The MVP uses self-reported monthly spend so you can get a fast audit without OAuth or invoice uploads.

### Why ask for email after the report?

The tool should prove value first. Email is only used to send the report and follow up when there is a meaningful savings opportunity.

### Is the public report private?

The public URL strips company, role, and email. It shows tools, plans, spend numbers, recommendations, and savings.

### Does the AI decide the recommendations?

No. The audit math is deterministic and source-backed. AI only turns the result into a short summary paragraph.

### When does Credex appear?

Credex is surfaced prominently when estimated savings exceed $500/month, because discounted credits become materially useful at that level.
